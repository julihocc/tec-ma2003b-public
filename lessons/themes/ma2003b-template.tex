  Where:
  \begin{itemize}
    \item $\mathbf{X}$: Observed variables $(p \times 1)$
    \item $\mathbf{\Lambda}$: Factor loadings matrix $(p \times k)$
    \item $\mathbf{F}$: Common factors $(k \times 1)$
    \item $\mathbf{\epsilon}$: Unique factors $(p \times 1)$
  \end{itemize}
